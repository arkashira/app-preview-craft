# Partner Targets

## Integration Roadmap

### Phase 1: Immediate (0-3 months)
1. **Figma**
   - **Rationale**: Figma is widely used by indie developers for UI/UX design. Integration allows users to import Figma designs directly into App Preview Craft for consistent branding.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Unlimited public files, 3 active private files.
   - **Integration effort**: S
   - **Value-add**: Solves the job of maintaining brand consistency across design and video assets.

2. **Canva**
   - **Rationale**: Canva is popular for graphic design and offers a vast library of templates. Integration enables users to import Canva graphics into their preview videos.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Access to free templates and basic design tools.
   - **Integration effort**: S
   - **Value-add**: Solves the job of easily incorporating custom graphics into videos.

### Phase 2: Short-term (3-6 months)
3. **Adobe Creative Cloud**
   - **Rationale**: Adobe Creative Cloud is a suite of professional design tools. Integration allows users to import Adobe assets (e.g., Photoshop, Illustrator) into their preview videos.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Limited access to basic tools and templates.
   - **Integration effort**: M
   - **Value-add**: Solves the job of leveraging professional design assets for high-quality videos.

4. **Unsplash**
   - **Rationale**: Unsplash provides high-quality stock photos. Integration enables users to easily add stock images to their preview videos.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Unlimited access to all photos.
   - **Integration effort**: S
   - **Value-add**: Solves the job of finding and incorporating high-quality stock images.

### Phase 3: Medium-term (6-12 months)
5. **Vimeo**
   - **Rationale**: Vimeo is a professional video hosting platform. Integration allows users to directly upload and share their preview videos on Vimeo.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 500MB weekly upload limit.
   - **Integration effort**: M
   - **Value-add**: Solves the job of professional video hosting and sharing.

6. **YouTube**
   - **Rationale**: YouTube is the largest video-sharing platform. Integration enables users to upload and share their preview videos on YouTube.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Unlimited uploads with certain resolution and length restrictions.
   - **Integration effort**: M
   - **Value-add**: Solves the job of reaching a broad audience with preview videos.

### Phase 4: Long-term (12+ months)
7. **App Store Connect API**
   - **Rationale**: Direct integration with the App Store Connect API allows users to upload their preview videos directly to the App Store.
   - **Free-tier limits**: N/A (requires Apple Developer account).
   - **Integration effort**: L
   - **Value-add**: Solves the job of streamlining the App Store submission process.

8. **Google Play Console API**
   - **Rationale**: Integration with the Google Play Console API enables users to upload their preview videos directly to the Google Play Store.
   - **Free-tier limits**: N/A (requires Google Developer account).
   - **Integration effort**: L
   - **Value-add**: Solves the job of streamlining the Google Play Store submission process.
