# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Indie iOS developer realizes the importance of high-quality App Store preview videos for app visibility and downloads.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about the complexity and cost of professional video creation.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration with low-quality videos, uncertainty about how to improve.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content on the impact of preview videos on app success.
- **Metric**: Number of unique visitors to landing page/blog posts.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Developer searches for solutions to create App Store preview videos.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelmed by the number of tools available, unsure which one is best suited for their needs.
- **User Emotions**: Confusion, skepticism about the effectiveness of different tools.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Clear comparison of features and benefits against competitors, case studies of successful indie developers.
- **Metric**: Time spent on product pages, engagement with comparison content.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Developer signs up for a free trial or demo of app-preview-craft.
- **Friction Points**: Complex onboarding process, lack of clear instructions.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety about learning a new tool.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Intuitive onboarding tutorial, quick start guide, and sample templates.
- **Metric**: Completion rate of onboarding process, time to first video creation.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Developer successfully creates and publishes a preview video using app-preview-craft.
- **Friction Points**: Limited customization options, integration issues with other tools.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction with the result, but potential frustration with limitations.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized support, advanced customization options, seamless integration with other development tools.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription, number of videos created and published.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Developer experiences increased app downloads and visibility after using app-preview-craft.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of advanced features for scaling video production.
- **User Emotions**: Desire to leverage the tool for more complex projects, frustration with feature limitations.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Access to premium templates, advanced editing features, and collaborative tools for team projects.
- **Metric**: Upsell rate to premium plans, number of additional team members added to the account.
